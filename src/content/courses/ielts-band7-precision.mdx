<ModuleHeader number={1} title="Band 6 to Band 7 gap" />

Band 7 performance requires fewer control errors, stronger collocations, and more natural coherence.

Precision comes from repeating targeted drills, not from adding complexity too early.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Common risk">
  Advanced words with weak collocation control can reduce clarity and lower scores.
</Callout>

<ModuleHeader number={2} title="Task execution strategy" />

Use timed cycles for reading, listening, writing, and speaking. Review errors after each cycle and classify by type.

A single error log makes recurring patterns visible and measurable.

<Accordion title="What to track in your error log">
  Track grammar control, lexical accuracy, response relevance, and timing discipline.
</Accordion>

<ModuleHeader number={3} title="High-value drills" />

Practice collocation upgrades, sentence transformation, and summary writing under time pressure.

Finish each practice block with one reflection sentence: what improved and what to fix next.
