<ModuleHeader number={1} title="Why carbon accounting matters" />

Carbon accounting helps organisations quantify emissions and identify reduction priorities. Consistent measurement enables better decisions over time.

A baseline period gives you a stable starting point. Without a baseline, progress claims are hard to compare year over year.

<Callout variant="info" title="Key term">
  <KeyTerm term="GHG Protocol" definition="A global standard for emissions accounting" /> is the common reference used by most organisations.
</Callout>

<ModuleHeader number={2} title="Scope 1, 2, and 3" />

Scope 1 covers direct emissions from controlled sources. Scope 2 covers purchased energy. Scope 3 covers value chain emissions.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Common trap">
  Scope 3 is often the largest category, but it is also the easiest to underestimate without supplier data.
</Callout>

<ModuleHeader number={3} title="Reporting and action" />

Build a repeatable data collection process first, then improve precision in each cycle. Reliable direction beats perfect but delayed reports.

Use activity data, apply emission factors, and document assumptions. Transparent notes make audits and reviews easier.
