Coverage gap (AU adv.)
Key insight: Australia has roughly 8.3× better specialist coverage per capita than Indonesia. The ratio gap is most extreme in Psychiatry (AU 1:5,400 vs ID 1:259,700), Emergency Medicine, and Geriatric Medicine. Indonesia's worst shortages are Nuclear Medicine (1:1.9M) and Geriatrics (1:1.6M) — under 200 doctors serving 285 million people.
