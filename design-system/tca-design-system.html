TCA · Design System

A bulk pantry, with a personality.

TCA is the visual & verbal system for Toko Curah Australia — a playful, warm, bilingual brand for a neighbourhood refill shop. Built on Nunito, anchored by navy & yellow, decorated with hand-drawn doodles.

06 · Voice & Tone

bilingual · warm · cheeky

Write like a friendly shopkeeper who knows their regulars. Plain English with occasional Bahasa Indonesia accents — never gimmicky, never translated word-for-word.

We say "Bring your jar, save 10%. Gampang banget." "Fluffy jasmine rice. The one your grandma swears by."
We don't say "Leverage our sustainable retail solutions." "Premium authentic exotic Asian rice."

12 · Logo

wordmark + Tc roundel

The TCA mark — a stylised storefront roundel. Navy is primary; on busy/dark surfaces use one of the brand-color variants. Clear space = ¼ of the mark's height on all sides.

Primary lockups
Navy · on yellow (default)
Navy · on cream
Yellow · on navy (reverse)
Color variants — swap navy for a brand hue

For moments when navy is too heavy or needs more warmth, the mark can shift to any one of the primary brand colors. Use one variant per surface — never mix two different colored marks on the same screen.

Usage rules
Do

Keep the mark on a flat brand surface, with generous clear space. Navy on yellow or cream is the default.

Don't

Don't rotate, skew, or place the mark on gradients, photos, or multicolor backgrounds. One surface color only.

14 · Do & Don't

quick checks
Halo Australia!
Do

Nunito Black, navy ink on yellow surface, hard shadow & a doodle nearby. Bilingual is a feature, not a footnote.

Halo Australia!
Don't

Rainbow gradients, script typefaces, drop shadows with blur. TCA is warm & honest — not Instagram-ad flashy.

15 · Tokens

copy-paste CSS

Drop these into a root stylesheet and you've got the whole system.

/* TCA Design System — v1.0 */
:root {
  /* Palette */
  --yellow: #F3C23A;
  --navy:   #1A3A7A;
  --red:    #D94331;
  --green:  #1E9B4D;
  --sky:    #18B4D9;
  --magenta:#D6398A;
  --cream:  #FBF4E4;
  --ink:    #1A1A1A;

  /* Type */
  --font-display: "Nunito", system-ui, sans-serif;
  --font-body:    "Nunito", system-ui, sans-serif;

  /* Radius */
  --r-sm: 8px;  --r-md: 16px;
  --r-lg: 22px; --r-xl: 36px;
  --r-pill: 999px;

  /* Signature shadow */
  --shadow-hard: 6px 6px 0 var(--navy);
}
